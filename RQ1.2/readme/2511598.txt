dvdprofiler app and tools


Goal is to provide a bunch of utilities to parse and handle collection.xml to add functionality and candy to dvdprofiler.
Additionally, a couchapp will be provided to play with data from dvdprofiler (http://www.invelos.com/ - great product - BUY it).


Getting started
- checkout
- configure dvdprofilerapp/dvdprofilerdata-import-batch-processing/src/main/resources/applicationContext.xml
	- for your couchdb
- run org.dvdprofilerapp.dvdprofilerimport.Import in dvdprofilerapp/dvdprofilerdata-import-batch-processing (in IDE - or assemvle all required libs first)
