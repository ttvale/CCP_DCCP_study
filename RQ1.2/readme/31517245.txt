A simple HTML markers implementation for Modest Maps. Eats [GeoJSON](http://www.geojson.org/),
produces HTML markers, positions them correctly.

## [Documentation](https://github.com/mapbox/markers.js/blob/gh-pages/API.md)
## [Home](http://mapbox.com/markers.js/)
## [Test](http://mapbox.com/markers.js/test/)
