Yogurt3D is a free - open source programming framework for 3d flash developers.

With your 3d you can bring your 3d content right into the browser, android platform and iOs platform easily. ( iOS and Android support comes with Air 3.2)

Yogurt3D uses it's own license agreement to give developers access to paid libraries future on. 
You can find the license agreement at http://www.yogurt3d.com/yogurt3d/downloads/yogurt3d-click-through-agreement.html

Any one that uses the engine or a fragment of it is assumed to accept the license terms.
